## Features

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Activity" icon="heartbeat" href="https://roteam.io" cta="View Feature">
    Real-time activity feeds showing all document changes, approvals, and team actions in one unified view.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Intranet" icon="book" href="https://roteam.io" cta="View Feature">
    Rich Notion-style editor with folders, drafts, and version history. Create handbooks, policies, and training guides.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Training" icon="graduation-cap" href="https://roteam.io" cta="View Feature">
    Link docs to trainings, quizzes, and certifications. Require reading before attendance or rank-up.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Approvals" icon="check" href="https://roteam.io" cta="View Feature">
    Multi-stage approval chains from draft to publish. HR, management, and owner approvals with full audit trails.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Logging" icon="notebook" href="https://roteam.io" cta="View Feature">
    Record warnings, promotions, demotions, and suspensions in your team's logbook.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Integrations" icon="arrows" href="https://roteam.io" cta="View Feature">
    Real-time notifications for approvals, acknowledgements, and policy updates directly to Discord.
  </Card>
</Columns>
