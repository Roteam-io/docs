The Logbook is your workspace's audit trail. Every significant action — promotions, demotions, strikes, suspensions, article changes, application reviews, and more — is automatically recorded here.

## Viewing the Logbook

Navigate to **Logbook** from the sidebar to see a chronological list of all actions in your workspace.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/logbook-overview.png" alt="Logbook overview" />
</Frame>

Each log entry shows:
- **Action type** — What happened (e.g., "Promotion", "Strike Issued", "Article Published")
- **Target** — Who or what was affected
- **Actor** — Who performed the action
- **Timestamp** — When it happened

## Filtering and Searching

Use the filters at the top of the logbook to narrow results:

- **Action Type** — Filter by specific action types (promotions, demotions, strikes, suspensions, etc.)
- **User** — Search for actions involving a specific member
- **Date Range** — Narrow to a specific time period

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/logbook-filters.png" alt="Logbook filters" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Use the logbook to review a member's complete history before making decisions about promotions, discipline, or role changes.
</Tip>

## Logged Actions

The logbook automatically records the following events:

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Promotion | A member was promoted to a higher rank |
| Demotion | A member was demoted to a lower rank |
| Strike | A disciplinary strike was issued |
| Suspension | A member was suspended |
| Article Created | A knowledgebase article was published |
| Article Updated | A knowledgebase article was modified |
| Application Reviewed | A staff application was reviewed |
| Member Joined | A new member joined the workspace |
| Member Left | A member left or was removed |

## Exporting Logs

To export your logbook data:

1. Apply any filters you need
2. Click the **Export** button
3. Choose your export format
4. The file will download with all matching log entries

<Note>
  The logbook is read-only. Entries cannot be edited or deleted, ensuring a reliable audit trail for your workspace.
</Note>
