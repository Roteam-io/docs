Announcements let you communicate important updates to your entire workspace. You can categorize posts, pin key messages, and let members react with emoji.

## Viewing Announcements

Navigate to **Announcements** from the sidebar to see a timeline of all posts. The most recent announcements appear at the top, with pinned announcements always displayed first.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/announcements-timeline.png" alt="Announcements timeline view" />
</Frame>

Use the **category filter** dropdown to narrow announcements by category (e.g., General, Update, Urgent, Event).

## Creating an Announcement

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Create">
    Click the **Create Announcement** button at the top of the announcements page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    - **Title** — A short, descriptive headline
    - **Content** — The body of your announcement. Supports rich text formatting.
    - **Category** — Select from your configured categories (e.g., General, Update, Urgent)
    - **Tags** — Add tags to help members find related announcements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Post** to publish the announcement immediately. All workspace members with view permissions will be able to see it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/announcements-create.png" alt="Create announcement dialog" />
</Frame>

## Pinning Announcements

Pinned announcements stay at the top of the timeline so they're always visible.

To pin an announcement, click the **three-dot menu** on any announcement card and select **Pin**. To unpin, repeat the same action and select **Unpin**.

<Tip>
  Use pinning for critical messages like policy changes, upcoming events, or ongoing issues that everyone needs to see.
</Tip>

## Reacting to Announcements

Members can react to announcements with emoji. Click any of the available reaction buttons below an announcement to add your reaction.

The available emoji reactions are configured by your workspace admin (default: 👍 🎉 ❤️ 🔥 👀).

## Managing Announcement Settings

Admins can customize announcements from **Settings > Announcements**.

### Custom Categories

Create categories to organize your announcements. Each category has a name and a color.

1. Go to **Settings > Announcements**
2. Under **Categories**, click **Add Category**
3. Enter a name (e.g., "Policy Update") and select a color
4. Click **Save**

### Custom Tags

Tags provide additional filtering and organization.

1. In **Settings > Announcements**, find the **Tags** section
2. Click **Add Tag** and enter a name
3. Tags are available when creating or editing announcements

### Allowed Reactions

Control which emoji reactions are available on announcements.

1. In **Settings > Announcements**, find **Allowed Emojis**
2. Add or remove emoji from the list
3. Changes apply to all announcements immediately

## Editing and Deleting

- **Edit**: Click the three-dot menu on an announcement and select **Edit** to modify the title, content, category, or tags.
- **Delete**: Click the three-dot menu and select **Delete**. You will be asked to confirm before the announcement is permanently removed.

<Note>
  Only users with the appropriate permissions can create, edit, or delete announcements. See [Permissions](/getting-started/quickstart#set-up-permissions) for details.
</Note>
