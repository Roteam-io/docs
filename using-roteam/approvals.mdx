Approvals provide a structured way to route requests through your organization. Whether it's a promotion, time-off request, or any other action that needs sign-off, the approval system ensures the right people review and authorize each request.

## How Approvals Work

When a member submits a request that requires approval (such as a promotion recommendation or time-off request), it enters the approval queue. Designated approvers are notified and can approve or deny the request.

## Approval Queue

Navigate to **Approvals** from the sidebar to see all pending items that need your attention.

Each item in the queue shows:
- **Type** — What kind of request it is (promotion, time off, etc.)
- **Submitted by** — Who made the request
- **Date** — When it was submitted
- **Status** — Pending, Approved, or Rejected

## Reviewing a Request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the request">
    Click on any pending item in the approval queue to see the full details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the information">
    Read through the request details, supporting information, and any notes from the requester.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve or Reject">
    - **Approve** — Click **Approve** to authorize the request. The associated action will be carried out.
    - **Reject** — Click **Reject** and optionally add a note explaining why. The requester will be notified.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Multi-Level Approvals

Some requests may require multiple levels of approval. In these cases:

1. The first approver reviews and approves
2. The request moves to the next level
3. Each level must approve before the action is finalized

If any level rejects the request, the entire request is denied.

## Approval History

View past approvals to see:
- What was approved or rejected
- Who made the decision
- When the decision was made
- Any notes or comments added during review

This creates a complete audit trail for accountability and compliance.
