The Org Chart displays your workspace's organizational hierarchy as an interactive visual tree. See how roles relate to each other and who reports to whom.

## Viewing the Org Chart

Navigate to **Org Chart** from the sidebar to see the visual hierarchy of your workspace.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/org-chart-overview.png" alt="Org chart visualization" />
</Frame>

### Navigation

- **Pan** — Click and drag to move around the chart
- **Zoom** — Scroll to zoom in and out
- **Search** — Use the search bar to find a specific role or member. The chart will auto-pan to the first match.

### What's Displayed

Each node in the chart represents a tracked role and shows:
- Role name
- Up to 5 members (sorted alphabetically)
- A "+X more" indicator if the role has more than 5 members

Clicking on a member navigates to their profile in the [Directory](/using-roteam/directory).

## Configuring the Org Chart

Set up your org chart's reporting relationships in **Settings > Org Chart**.

### Setting Up Reporting Relationships

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Org Chart settings">
    Go to **Settings > Org Chart**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a reporting rule">
    Click **Add Rule** to define a parent-child relationship between roles. For example, "Junior Staff" reports to "Senior Staff".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the relationship type">
    Select the reports-to type:
    - **Role-to-Role** — An entire role reports to another role (e.g., all Moderators report to Senior Moderators)
    - **Individual-to-User** — A specific member reports to a specific person
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to update the chart. Changes are reflected immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/org-chart-settings.png" alt="Org chart settings" />
</Frame>

### Tracked Roles

Only roles marked as **tracked** appear in the org chart. To manage tracked roles:

1. Go to **Settings > Members & Roles**
2. Toggle roles on or off under **Tracked Roles**
3. Untracked roles are hidden from the org chart, leaderboard, and activity tracking

<Tip>
  Start by setting up your top-level roles first, then work downward. This makes it easier to visualize the hierarchy as you build it.
</Tip>

<Note>
  The org chart updates automatically when members change roles in your Roblox group. After a group sync, new members will appear in their respective roles.
</Note>
