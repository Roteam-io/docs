Reports let you generate and export detailed data about your workspace. Analyze activity trends, attendance patterns, discipline records, and more with customizable report parameters.

## Accessing Reports

Navigate to **Reports** from the sidebar to access the report builder.

  <img src="/images/reports-overview.png" alt="Reports page" />
</Frame>

## Report Types

### Activity Reports

Analyze member engagement and time spent in-game.

- **Activity Summary** — Total minutes, active days, and AFK percentage per member
- **Activity Trends** — Engagement over time with charts showing rising or declining activity
- **Top Performers** — Members with the most activity in a given period

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/reports-activity.png" alt="Activity report" />
</Frame>

### Attendance Reports

Track presence at sessions and events.

- **Session Attendance** — Who attended which sessions and in what role
- **Attendance Rate** — Percentage of events attended per member
- **Host/Co-Host Summary** — How many sessions each member has hosted or co-hosted

### Discipline Reports

Review disciplinary actions across your workspace.

- **Strikes Summary** — All strikes issued with member, reason, and date
- **Suspension History** — Active and past suspensions
- **Demotion Log** — All recorded demotions with details

### Custom Reports

Build reports with your own parameters:
- Select which data fields to include
- Apply filters by member, role, date range, or action type
- Choose sorting and grouping options

## Generating a Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select report type">
    Choose from the available report templates or create a custom report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set parameters">
    Configure the report:
    - **Date Range** — Select the time period to analyze
    - **Members** — All members, specific roles, or individual users
    - **Filters** — Additional criteria to narrow results
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate Report**. The report will be processed and displayed as a table with charts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/reports-generate.png" alt="Report generation form" />
</Frame>

## Viewing Report Results

Generated reports are displayed as:

- **Data Table** — Sortable and searchable table of results
- **Charts** — Visual representations of trends and distributions (where applicable)
- **Summary Statistics** — Key metrics at the top of the report

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/reports-results.png" alt="Report results view" />
</Frame>

## Exporting Reports

Export your report data for use in external tools:

1. Generate your report
2. Click the **Export** button
3. Choose your format (CSV, etc.)
4. The file will download with all report data

<Tip>
  Schedule regular reports (e.g., weekly activity summaries) using [Automation](/using-roteam/automation) to stay on top of your workspace metrics without manual effort.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Report availability may vary by workspace plan. Some advanced report types are available on higher-tier plans.
</Note>
