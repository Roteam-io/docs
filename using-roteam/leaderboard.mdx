The Leaderboard ranks your workspace members by activity and performance. Use it to recognize top contributors and motivate your team.

## Viewing the Leaderboard

Navigate to **Leaderboard** from the sidebar. The leaderboard displays a podium for the top 3 performers and a paginated list below.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/leaderboard-overview.png" alt="Leaderboard with podium and list" />
</Frame>

### Podium

The top 3 members are displayed prominently:
- **1st place** — Gold
- **2nd place** — Silver
- **3rd place** — Bronze

### Your Rank

If you're not on the current page of results, a **"Your Rank"** card shows your position so you always know where you stand.

## Leaderboard Modes

Switch between two ranking modes:

### Most Time

Ranks members by total activity minutes spent in-game. This is the default mode.

### Top Sessions

Ranks members by their involvement in sessions and events, including:
- **Hosted** — Sessions where the member was the primary host
- **Co-hosted** — Sessions where the member was a co-host
- **Assisted** — Sessions where the member had a role assignment

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/leaderboard-sessions.png" alt="Sessions leaderboard mode" />
</Frame>

## Time Ranges

Filter the leaderboard by different time periods:
- **Daily** — Today's top performers
- **Weekly** — This week's rankings
- **1 Month** — Last 30 days
- **3 Months** — Last 90 days
- **All Time** — Total accumulated activity

Activity and session leaderboards can have independent time ranges.

## Employee of the Week (EOTW)

The Employee of the Week feature highlights a standout member with a banner at the top of the leaderboard.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/leaderboard-eotw.png" alt="Employee of the Week banner" />
</Frame>

The EOTW banner displays the winner's name, avatar, and vote count.

## Leaderboard Settings

Configure the leaderboard in **Settings > Leaderboard**:

- **Activity Range** — Default time range for the activity leaderboard
- **Session Range** — Default time range for the sessions leaderboard
- **Tracked Roles** — Only members in tracked roles appear on the leaderboard
- **Display Count** — How many members to show on the public site leaderboard

<Tip>
  Share the leaderboard on your [public site](/using-roteam/sites) to motivate members even when they're not logged into the dashboard.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Leaderboard rankings update automatically as new activity data is recorded. There may be a short delay between in-game activity and leaderboard updates.
</Note>
