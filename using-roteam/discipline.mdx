The Discipline tools help you maintain accountability in your workspace. Issue strikes for rule violations, demote members when necessary, and suspend members who need to be temporarily removed from activity.

## Strikes

Strikes are formal warnings recorded against a member. They create a permanent record in the [Logbook](/using-roteam/logbook) for accountability and transparency.

### Issuing a Strike

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Discipline">
    Go to **Discipline > Strikes** from the sidebar, or open a member's profile in the [Directory](/using-roteam/directory).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Issue Strike">
    Click **Issue Strike** and fill in:
    - **Member** — Select the member receiving the strike
    - **Reason** — Describe why the strike is being issued
    - **Date** — When the infraction occurred (defaults to today)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review the details and click **Submit**. The strike is recorded immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/discipline-strike.png" alt="Issue strike form" />
</Frame>

### Viewing Strikes

The Strikes list shows all issued strikes with:
- Member name and avatar
- Reason for the strike
- Date issued
- Who issued the strike

Use the search bar and filters to find strikes by member, date, or issuer.

### Deleting a Strike

If a strike was issued in error, authorized users can delete it:
1. Find the strike in the list
2. Click the **Delete** button
3. Confirm the deletion

<Warning>
  Deleting a strike removes it permanently. Consider whether the strike should be kept for historical records before deleting.
</Warning>

## Demotions

Demotions lower a member's rank in your workspace and Roblox group.

### Recording a Demotion

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Discipline">
    Go to **Discipline > Demotions** from the sidebar, or open the member's profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Demote">
    Click **Demote** and provide:
    - **Member** — The member being demoted
    - **New Rank** — The rank they will be demoted to
    - **Reason** — Why the demotion is happening
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review and submit. The demotion is logged in the Logbook.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/discipline-demotion.png" alt="Demotion form" />
</Frame>

<Note>
  If [Auto Ranking](/using-roteam/advancement) is enabled, the rank change will be synced to your Roblox group automatically. If not, you will need to update the member's rank manually in the Roblox group.
</Note>

### Viewing Demotions

The Demotions list shows all recorded demotions with the member, previous rank, new rank, reason, and date.

## Suspensions

Suspensions temporarily remove a member from active participation. You can set a specific end date or leave it open-ended.

### Suspending a Member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Discipline">
    Go to **Discipline > Suspensions** from the sidebar, or open the member's profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Suspend">
    Click **Suspend** and provide:
    - **Member** — The member being suspended
    - **Reason** — Why they are being suspended
    - **Duration** — How long the suspension lasts (e.g., 3 days, 1 week, 30 days)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review and submit. The member's suspension status is active immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/discipline-suspension.png" alt="Suspension form" />
</Frame>

### Viewing Active Suspensions

The Suspensions list shows:
- Member name
- Reason for suspension
- **Suspended Until** date
- Who issued the suspension

### Adjusting a Suspension

If you need to extend or shorten a suspension:

1. Find the suspension in the list
2. Click **Adjust**
3. Set the new end date
4. Save the change

### Suspension Expiration

Suspensions are automatically lifted when the end date is reached. The member's status returns to normal without any manual action required.

<Tip>
  Use strikes for minor infractions and suspensions for more serious issues. Keep demotions for cases where a member is no longer performing at their current rank level.
</Tip>
