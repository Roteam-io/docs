Time Off lets members request leave from their workspace responsibilities. Managers can review, approve, or deny requests, and track leave history across the team.

## Requesting Time Off

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Time Off">
    Go to **Time Off** from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Request Time Off">
    Click the **Request Time Off** button to open the request form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    - **Leave Type** — Select the type of leave (e.g., Vacation, Sick Leave, Personal, LOA)
    - **Start Date** — When your leave begins
    - **End Date** — When your leave ends
    - **Reason** — Explain why you're requesting time off
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit**. Your request will be sent to the designated approvers for review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tracking Your Requests

After submitting, you can see the status of your requests on the Time Off page:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Your request is waiting for review |
| **Approved** | Your request has been approved |
| **Rejected** | Your request was denied (check reviewer notes for details) |

## Approving Time Off (Managers)

Users with the **Approve** permission for time-off requests can review and process incoming requests.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View pending requests">
    Navigate to **Time Off** or **Approvals** to see pending time-off requests in a table view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the request">
    Click on a request to see the full details — leave type, dates, reason, and the member's leave history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve or Reject">
    - **Approve** — Click **Approve** to grant the request
    - **Reject** — Click **Reject** and add a note explaining the reason for denial
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/time-off-approve.png" alt="Time off approval view" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Review the member's upcoming schedule and existing leave before making a decision to avoid conflicts.
</Tip>

## Leave Types

Your workspace admin configures which leave types are available. Common types include:

- **Vacation** — Planned time away
- **Sick Leave** — Illness or medical needs
- **Personal** — Personal matters
- **LOA (Leave of Absence)** — Extended time away

To configure leave types, go to **Settings** and update the leave type options.

## Leave History

View a complete history of all time-off requests — both your own and (with permissions) your team's. The history shows:
- Member name
- Leave type
- Date range
- Status
- Reviewer and review date
- Reviewer notes (if any)

<Note>
  Time-off requests integrate with the [Approvals](/using-roteam/approvals) system. Approvers are notified when new requests are submitted.
</Note>
