The Hiring feature lets you build custom application forms for staff recruitment. Share forms with candidates, collect structured responses, and manage your review pipeline.

## Viewing Applications

Navigate to **Hiring** from the sidebar to see all your application forms. Each card shows:
- Form title and description
- Status badge (Draft, Published, or Archived)
- Number of submissions received

## Creating an Application Form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Create Application">
    Click the **Create Application** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up the basics">
    - **Title** — Name of the application (e.g., "Moderator Application", "Staff Application")
    - **Description** — Brief explanation of the role and what you're looking for
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add form fields">
    Build your form by adding fields. Available field types include:
    - **Text** — Short text input (name, username, etc.)
    - **Textarea** — Long text input (essays, explanations)
    - **Select** — Dropdown with predefined options
    - **Checkbox** — Yes/no or multiple choice options

    For each field, you can set:
    - Field label (the question)
    - Whether it's required
    - Placeholder text
    - Options (for select/checkbox types)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure review stages">
    Define the stages each application goes through:
    - Example: "Initial Review" → "Interview" → "Final Decision"
    - Applications move through stages as reviewers process them
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set cooldown">
    Configure a **cooldown period** (in days) to prevent candidates from re-applying too frequently. For example, a 30-day cooldown means a rejected applicant must wait 30 days before applying again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Set the form status to **Published** to make it live. Draft forms are only visible to admins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sharing Application Forms

Once published, share your application form through multiple channels:

### Copy Link
Click the **Share** button on any published form and select **Copy Link**. Share the URL directly with candidates.

### QR Code
Generate a QR code that candidates can scan to access the form. Great for in-game or social media sharing.

### Email Invite
Send personalized email invitations:
1. Click **Share > Email Invite**
2. Enter the recipient's name and email address
3. Click **Send**

## Reviewing Applications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the form">
    Click on a published application form to see its submissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse submissions">
    View all submitted applications with their current review stage and status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review a submission">
    Click on a submission to read the candidate's responses. Review each answer and any attached information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move through stages">
    Advance the application through your configured review stages (e.g., from "Initial Review" to "Interview"). Add notes at each stage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make a decision">
    Accept or reject the application at the final stage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Default Fields

Save time by creating reusable field templates:

1. Go to **Settings > Applications**
2. Under **Default Fields**, create fields you use frequently
3. When creating a new form, add default fields with one click instead of rebuilding them

## Managing Forms

- **Edit** — Click on a draft form to modify its fields, stages, or settings
- **Archive** — Archive forms that are no longer accepting applications. Existing submissions are preserved.
- **Delete** — Permanently remove a form and all its submissions

<Tip>
  Start with a simple form and iterate. You can always add more fields or stages as your hiring process evolves.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Published forms can be shared on your [public site](/using-roteam/sites) so candidates can apply directly from your community's website.
</Note>
