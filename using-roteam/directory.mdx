The Directory gives you a centralized view of all members in your workspace. Browse profiles, search by name or role, and manage your team structure.

## Browsing Members

Navigate to **Directory** from the sidebar to see all workspace members. Members are displayed with their:
- Profile avatar
- Display name and Roblox username
- Current rank in your Roblox group
- Permission group(s)
- Join date

## Searching and Filtering

Use the search bar at the top to find members by:
- **Name** — Search by display name or Roblox username
- **Role** — Filter by Roblox group rank
- **Permission Group** — Filter by assigned permission group
- **Tags** — Filter by custom tags

## Viewing Member Profiles

Click on any member to view their full profile. A member profile includes:

- **Basic Info** — Name, avatar, Roblox username, current rank
- **Activity Summary** — Total minutes, active days, AFK percentage
- **Session History** — Events they've attended or hosted
- **Logbook History** — Promotions, strikes, and other logged actions
- **Course Progress** — Assigned courses and completion status

## Managing Members

Depending on your permissions, you can take actions directly from a member's profile:

- **Promote** — Submit a promotion recommendation
- **Demote** — Record a demotion
- **Strike** — Issue a disciplinary strike
- **Suspend** — Place the member on suspension
- **Assign Course** — Assign a training course
- **Update Permission Group** — Change their permission group assignment

<Tip>
  The Directory is the quickest way to get a full picture of any team member — their activity, history, and current standing — all in one place.
</Tip>

## Tracked Roles

Only members in **tracked roles** appear in the directory and on leaderboards. To configure tracked roles:

1. Go to **Settings > Members & Roles**
2. Under **Tracked Roles**, select which Roblox group ranks should be tracked
3. Members in untracked roles will not appear in activity tracking or leaderboards

<Note>
  Changes to tracked roles take effect after the next member sync. You can trigger a manual sync from **Settings > Workspace Info**.
</Note>
