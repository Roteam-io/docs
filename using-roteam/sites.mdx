Sites lets you create a public website for your community hosted on a `.roteamsite.io` subdomain. Showcase your team, publish schedules, display leaderboards, and accept applications — all without writing any code.

## Enabling Your Site

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Sites settings">
    Go to **Settings > Sites** from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the site">
    Click **Enable Site** to activate your public page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your site">
    Your site is live at `yourworkspace.roteamsite.io`. Copy the URL or click **Visit** to open it in a new tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customizing Your Site

### Theme

Configure the look and feel of your public site:

- **Mode** — Choose between **Light** or **Dark** mode
- **Color** — Select from 10 theme color options to match your community's branding

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/sites-theme.png" alt="Site theme settings" />
</Frame>

### Menu Items

Toggle which sections appear on your public site:

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Announcements** | Public announcement feed |
| **Leaderboard** | Top performers displayed publicly |
| **Applications** | Open application forms for recruitment |
| **Members** | Team member directory |

Each section can be individually enabled or disabled.

## Announcements Settings

When announcements are enabled on your site:

- **Count** — Choose how many announcements to display on the public page

## Leaderboard Settings

When the leaderboard is enabled:

- **Activity Count** — How many members to show on the activity leaderboard
- **Session Count** — How many members to show on the sessions leaderboard
- **Activity Time Range** — Period for activity rankings (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.)
- **Session Time Range** — Period for session rankings
- **Employee of the Week** — Toggle to show or hide the EOTW banner

## Members Settings

Configure how team members are displayed on your public site:

- **Display Mode** — Show all members, filter by permission group, by role, or select specific users
- **Separate by Rank** — Group members by their rank, or show as a flat list

## Applications on Your Site

When applications are enabled, your published [application forms](/using-roteam/hiring) are displayed on the site. Candidates can browse open positions and apply directly.

## Disabling Your Site

To take your site offline:

1. Go to **Settings > Sites**
2. Click **Disable Site**
3. You'll be asked to type your workspace name to confirm
4. The site will be taken offline immediately

<Warning>
  Disabling your site removes public access immediately. Any shared links to your site will stop working.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  Use your public site URL in your Roblox group description, social media bios, and Discord server to drive traffic to your community page.
</Tip>
