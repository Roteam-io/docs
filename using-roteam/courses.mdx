Courses let you create structured training programs for your team. Build sequential modules, assign courses to members, track progress, and award certifications on completion.

## Browsing Courses

Navigate to **Courses** from the sidebar. The courses page has three tabs:

- **My Courses** — Courses assigned to you that are in progress
- **Completed** — Courses you've finished
- **Browse** — All published courses available in your workspace

  <img src="/images/courses-overview.png" alt="Courses overview with tabs" />
</Frame>

Each course card shows:
- Course title and description
- Number of modules
- Your progress (percentage complete)
- Due date (if assigned with a deadline)
- Status badges: overdue (red), approaching deadline (amber), completed (green)

## Taking a Course

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the course">
    Click on a course card to open it. You'll see a list of all modules in order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete modules">
    Work through each module by reading the content and completing any required actions. Click **Mark as Complete** when you finish a module.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your progress">
    Your progress bar updates as you complete modules. If the course is sequential, you must complete modules in order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn certification">
    Once all modules are completed, you'll receive a certification. View your certifications from the **Completed** tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating a Course

Users with course management permissions can create new courses.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Create Course">
    From the courses page, click **Create Course**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set course details">
    - **Title** — Name of the course
    - **Description** — What the course covers
    - **Sequential** — Toggle on to require modules to be completed in order. Toggle off to allow members to complete modules in any order.
    - **Self-Enrollment** — Toggle on to let members enroll themselves without being assigned
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add modules">
    Click **Add Module** to create training modules. Each module can include:
    - Title and description
    - Rich text content (instructions, guides, policies)
    - Links to external resources
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set status">
    - **Draft** — Course is being built and not visible to members
    - **Published** — Course is live and available
    - **Archived** — Course is no longer active but records are preserved
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assigning Courses

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the course">
    Navigate to the course you want to assign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Assign">
    Click the **Assign** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select members">
    Choose the members or groups you want to assign the course to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a due date (optional)">
    Add a deadline for course completion. Members will see the due date on their course card and receive reminders as it approaches.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use self-enrollment for optional training and manual assignment for required courses. This gives you flexibility in how training is delivered.
</Tip>

## Tracking Progress

From the course management view, you can see:
- How many members are assigned
- Individual progress for each member
- Who has completed the course and when
- Who is overdue

## Certifications

When a member completes all modules in a course, they automatically receive a certification. Certifications are visible on the member's profile and in the **Completed** tab.

<Note>
  Archived courses preserve all existing assignments and certifications but prevent new enrollments.
</Note>
