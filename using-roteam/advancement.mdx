Advancement covers everything related to rank progression in your workspace — from crowdsourced promotion recommendations to fully automated ranking based on activity metrics.

## Promotion Recommendations

Any member with the right permissions can recommend someone for a promotion.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit a recommendation">
    Navigate to the member's profile in the [Directory](/using-roteam/directory) or the Advancement page. Click **Recommend for Promotion**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide details">
    - **Member** — Who you're recommending
    - **Reason** — Why they deserve a promotion
    - **Rating** — Your confidence level in the recommendation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit**. The recommendation will appear in the recommendations queue for managers to review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Advancement page shows recommendations organized by:
- **Top** — Most recommended members

## Reviewing and Approving Promotions

Managers and users with approval permissions can review recommendations and process promotions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review recommendations">
    Open the Advancement page and browse pending recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve or deny">
    Click on a recommendation to review the details. Choose to **Approve** (promote the member) or **Deny** the recommendation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm rank change">
    If approved, confirm the new rank. If Auto Ranking is enabled, the rank change will be synced to your Roblox group automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/advancement-approve.png" alt="Promotion approval dialog" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
  If Auto Ranking is not enabled, you will need to manually update the member's rank in your Roblox group after approving a promotion.
</Warning>

## Promotion History

View a complete log of all promotions and demotions in the **History** tab. Each entry shows:
- Member promoted/demoted
- Previous rank and new rank
- Who approved the change
- Date and time

## Auto Ranking

Auto Ranking automates rank changes based on configurable criteria. Members are automatically promoted or demoted when they meet (or no longer meet) the defined thresholds.

### Setting Up Auto Ranking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to settings">
    Go to **Settings > Advancement** or find Auto Ranking in the Advancement section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Auto Ranking">
    Toggle the **Auto Ranking** switch on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your Roblox API key">
    Enter your Roblox Open Cloud API key. This is required for Roteam to update ranks in your Roblox group.

    To get an API key:
    1. Visit the [Roblox Creator Dashboard](https://create.roblox.com/credentials)
    2. Create a new API key with **Group** permissions
    3. Grant **Read** and **Write** access to your group's membership
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure rank conditions">
    For each rank, define the promotion criteria:
    - **Activity Minutes** — Minimum total minutes in-game
    - **Session Count** — Minimum number of sessions attended/hosted
    - **Days Since Last Promotion** — Cooldown period between promotions

    Example:
    | From Rank | To Rank | Minutes Required | Sessions Required | Cooldown |
    |-----------|---------|-----------------|-------------------|----------|
    | Trainee | Junior Staff | 500 | 10 | 7 days |
    | Junior Staff | Staff | 2,000 | 30 | 14 days |
    | Staff | Senior Staff | 5,000 | 75 | 30 days |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and verify">
    Save your configuration. Use the **Test** button to verify your API key is working and rank changes can be processed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/advancement-auto-ranking.png" alt="Auto ranking configuration" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Start with conservative thresholds and adjust over time. You can always lower requirements, but demoting members who were auto-promoted too quickly can cause friction.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Auto Ranking runs on a periodic schedule. There may be a short delay between a member meeting the criteria and their rank being updated.
</Note>
